My Process

From wireframes to final design, below is a detailed look at how I run a typical project.

Step 1 - Getting Started

Brief

To get a clear picture of what you need I start every project by receiving an initial brief of what you want. Having this gives me enough context to properly provide you with a proposal and ultimately tailor my services to your project goals.

Initial Chat

The next step is to have a quick 15-minute call so we can properly introduce ourselves and make sure we're both on the same page. This is the perfect time to let me know anything I might need to be aware of during the project.

Step 2 - Design

Wireframes

Having a good set of wireframes is the bedrock of any successful project. This is where we hammer down the layout and general functionality while always making sure the user is the primary focus.

Designs

This is where I apply the paint to your requirements. I'll take the concepts we've agreed on in the wireframes to make them look usable, modern and clean.
